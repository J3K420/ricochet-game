# 🎯 RICOCHET — a slingshot space brawler

A mouse-only neon arcade game. You are a glowing orb. **Pull back, aim, release** — fling yourself across
the arena and ricochet through swarms of enemies. One perfect shot can wipe the screen. Every launch is
a deliberate, aimed slingshot. No auto-fire, no passive systems — just you, the pull, and the smash.

## How to play

Open **`index.html`** in any modern browser. No install, no build.

### Controls — mouse only
- **Press & hold the left mouse button** on your orb, then **drag away** to pull back like a slingshot.
  The further you pull, the more power. An aiming line and trajectory preview show where you'll go.
- **Release** to fling yourself in the opposite direction. You bounce off walls and smash through enemies.
- **Ram enemies fast to destroy them** — a slow nudge just bounces. Chain multiple kills in a single
  launch for huge **combo multipliers**.
- Wait for your orb to slow down, then line up your next shot.

### Powers (all deliberate — earned, never automatic)
- **Overcharge** — hold the pull at max power for a beat; your next fling is faster, pierces harder, and
  leaves a damaging magenta afterimage trail.
- **Echo-orbs** — flick the mid-air pulse during a combo to split off 2–3 echo orbs that smash too.
- **Supernova** — combos fill the NOVA meter; when it reads READY, an overcharge-release (or charged pulse)
  detonates a screen-clearing blast.
- **Time-freeze** — once NOVA is half-full, *double-tap* the pull to freeze every enemy and projectile
  while you line up a perfect multi-smash.

### Rampage → Overdrive
Kills and combos fill the RAMPAGE meter. Fill it and you trigger **OVERDRIVE**: faster launches, snappier
recovery, **×4 score**, hotter visuals, and the music kicks into a driving bed. The whole screen heats up
as your combos climb.

### New threats
Sentinels (turrets that shoot — dodge the bolts), Lancers (charge and lunge — sidestep the locked line),
Wardens (rotating shields — bank-shot the rear gap or crack with a charged hit), Hives (spawn swarms of
Wisps), and the **Overseer** — a 3-phase boss every 8th wave.

### Goal
Survive escalating waves, build RAMPAGE into OVERDRIVE, and chase the highest score with massive
ricochet combos.
